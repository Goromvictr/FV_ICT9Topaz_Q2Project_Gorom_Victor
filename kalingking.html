Kalingking

Ingredients

  • 2 large yellow sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced into thick matchsticks
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Sugaw
  • Oil

Instructions

  • In a bowl, combine together flour, baking powder, milk, salt and egg. Mix it well until smooth.
  • Add the sweet potatoes.
  • Heat the oil in a pan then arrange a clump of sweet potatoes, gently place it in the hot oil and flatten it with a spatula. Do it with the remaining sweet potato mixture but don't overcrowd.
  • Turn the heat to a low then fry until golden brown in colour.
  • Place in a paper towel lined plate prior to serving to remove excess oil.
  • Sprinkle sugar on top before serving for taste.
