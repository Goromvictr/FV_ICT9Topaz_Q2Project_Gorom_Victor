Pansit Bato

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb. Pancit Bato
  • 2 cups chopped cabbage
  • 1 medium carrot (julienned)
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 6 ounces pork (sliced)
  • 6 ounces medium (shrimp shell and head removed)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions

  • Heat the cooking oil in a cooking pot.
  • Once the oil is hot, saute the garlic and onion.
  • Add the sliced pork then cook for 3 minutes.
  • Pour-in the soy sauce, fish sauce, and chicken broth. Let it boil. Simmer for 20 minutes. Add water if needed.
  • Add the ground black pepper then stir.
  • Put-in the shrimp, snap peas, and carrots and cook for a minute.
  • Add the cabbage and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Put-in the pancit bato, then stir and cook until all the liquid is absorbed. The noodles should be soft. Otherwise, add a bit more water to soften the noodles.
  • Transfer to a serving plate, share, and enjoy!
