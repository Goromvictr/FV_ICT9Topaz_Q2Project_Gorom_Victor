Suman sa Ibos

Ingredients

  • 4 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 1/4 tablespoons sea salt
  • 2 1/4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  • Make the buri container by swirling the buri leaves on a mold that's about 1 1/2 inches in width. Lock the cylindrical leaf container that you molded to prevent the leaf from swirling back.
  • Wash the rice and then place in a large bowl.
  • Pour-in water then let the rice absorb it.
  • Pour-in the coconut milk and then add the salt, gently mix and let it stand for about 10 minutes.
  • Scoop the mixture and transfer it to the buri container and then seal. Set it aside.
  • Arrange the suman in a large cooking pot, pour-in about a quart of water. Let it boil.
  • Adjust the heat to low-medium and let the suman cook for 60 to 90 minutes.
  • Remove the suman from the cooking pot. Let the temperature cool it down.
  • Serve with ripe mangoes on the side (optional). Share and enjoy!
