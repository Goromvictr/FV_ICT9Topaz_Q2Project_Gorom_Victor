Sinanglay na Tilapia

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces tilapia (cleaned and scales removed)
  • 4 pieces bok choy or (pechay leaves)
  • 4 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1 piece diced onion
  • 2 pieces diced tomato
  • 1 thumb chopped ginger
  • 3 pieces long green chili
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  • Mix the garlic, onion, tomatoes, and ginger in a bowl.
  • Stuff the tilapia with the garlic, onion, and ginger mixture.
  • Wrap the bok choy around the tilapia.
  • Secure the bok choy by tying a kitchen twine around the tilapia. (Optional)
  • Arrange the wrapped tilapia in a wide cooking pan, then pour coconut milk and water. Sprinkle salt, cover, and turn on the heat.
  • Cook the fish on medium heat for 20 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat, remove the cooking twine and transfer the fish to a serving plate.
  • Serve warm and enjoy!
